www.sps-aviation.com/story/?id=2160&h=Aleksey-Maresyev-1916-2001. This article provides a more in-depth story of Alexey Maresyev's life. It provides more details to his early life than on any other page we have found. It also provides information as to how he got the idea to become a pilot and why he wanted to fly. It tells his story of working through the ranks, first become a Soviet air technician then a pilot. Alexey's fights before his crash are also discussed, as well as his crash and flights after he was successfully in returning to flight. The last point made is that after he retired he became a member of the Supreme Soviet and helped out other veterans and advocated for disabled rights.
